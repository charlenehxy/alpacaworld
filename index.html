Difference between Llamas and Alpacas.

Size

Llamas are larger than Alpacas. An adult llama weighs between 130-200kg while an adult alpaca weighs between 50-80kg.

Personality

Llamas are confident and brave animals while Alpacas are very gentle and shy.

Wool

Llamas have coarser wool which are usually white in color. Alpacas have longer and finer wool which are available in 22 colors.
