Difference between Llamas and Alpacas.

Size

  • Llamas are larger than Alpacas.
  • An adult llama weighs between 130-200kg while an adult alpaca weighs between 50-80kg.

    • Personality

  • Llamas are confident and brave animals.
  • Alpacas are very gentle and shy.

Wool

  • Llamas have relatively coarse wool.
    • Wools are usually white in color, but also available in black, shades of brown, red and beige.
  • Alpacas have longer and finer wool.
    • Wools are available in 22 colors, including black, white, pinto, brown, red, fawn, rose and gray.
